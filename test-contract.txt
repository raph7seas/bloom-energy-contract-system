BLOOM ENERGY POWER PURCHASE AGREEMENT

This Power Purchase Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on January 1, 2024, by and between:

CUSTOMER: Acme Manufacturing Corp.
PROVIDER: Bloom Energy Corporation

WHEREAS, Customer requires reliable electrical power for its manufacturing operations;
WHEREAS, Provider has the capability to install and maintain fuel cell power generation systems;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

1. SYSTEM SPECIFICATIONS
   - Capacity: 2000 kW
   - Technology: Solid Oxide Fuel Cell
   - Installation: Customer premises
   - Term: 20 years

2. FINANCIAL TERMS
   - Base Rate: $0.15/kWh
   - Annual Escalation: 2.5%
   - Microgrid Adder: $0.02/kWh
   - Payment Terms: Net 30 days

3. OPERATING CONDITIONS
   - Minimum Demand: 500 kW
   - Maximum Demand: 2000 kW
   - Efficiency Warranty: 60%
   - Output Warranty: 95%

4. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
   - Voltage: 480V
   - Components: RI, AC, UC systems
   - Grid Connection: Utility interconnection
   - Monitoring: Real-time remote monitoring

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of California and any disputes shall be subject to binding arbitration.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement on the date first written above.
